Dark Creatures With A Golden Ratio

Every CryptoBat trait has been creatively directed by Ozzy Osbourne. Unlike some of Ozzy’s previous bat friends, these ones live safely in the metaverse and are not edible… yet.

But we went even further… to achieve the perfect composition, we followed THE FIBONACCI SEQUENCE (ALSO KNOWN AS THE GOLDEN RATIO) making CryptoBatz artwork oddly pleasing to the eye and beautiful enough that you could hang on the wall next to that photo of grandma.

ANCIENTBATZ is a life changing treasure hunt reserved exclusively for CryptoBatz holders.

Where CryptoBatz can only bite once, AncientBatz are able to bite up to ninety-nine times, granting them an unrivaled power to each breed 99 MutantBatz.

AncientBatz are each custom designed by some of the most renowned artists in the world, meaning that beyond utility, the holder also possesses a piece of extremely rare art.

We’ve scattered AncientBatz around the world in hidden locations. They could be up a mountain, deep in a forest, underwater or even stuck to the ceiling of a dive bar toilet… the common theme? They’re not easy to find.

Week by week we will release hints and clues to our most engaged community, slowly revealing the locations of AncientBatz and making them easier to discover as time goes on.

If you are one of the lucky handful to discover an AncientBat, you will simply scan a QR code revealing a special wallet that can only be unlocked using your CryptoBat.
